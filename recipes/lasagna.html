Lasagna Recipe

Description:

Lasagna is an old world dish. It is a classic made of pasta, meat, and tomato sauce. A layered meat cake, if you will.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Meat
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Layer the pasta, meat, and tomato sauce to the top of your pan
  2. Preheat oven
  3. Cook in oven for 45 minutes
  4. Remove and let sit for 20 before serving

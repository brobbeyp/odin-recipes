Beer Recipe

Description:

Beer, the water of the gods.

Ingredients

  • Wheat
  • Barley
  • Hops
  • Yeast

Steps

  1. Add your wheat, barley, hops, yeast, and any additional flavorings to a barrel
  2. Let ingredients ferment in barrel
  3. Taste periodically until desired flavor achieved
  4. Enjoy responsibly

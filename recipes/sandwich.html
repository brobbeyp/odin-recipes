Sandwich Recipe

Description:

Sandwiches are a staple of any healthy man's diet. Simple, yet complex. Ranging from small to record breaking sizes.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Meat
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Apply condiments and sauces to either side of your favorite bread
  2. Optionally, toast bread to desired level
  3. Add meat and cheese in layers, as preferred, to one slice
  4. Add your second slice of bread
